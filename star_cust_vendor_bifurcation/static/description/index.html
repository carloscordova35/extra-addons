Customer Vendor Bifurcation

The module introduces a user-friendly checkbox feature for easy categorization of records into customer and vendor segments, streamlining data management and enhancing user experience.

Customer/Vendor Configuration

In the customer form, you can designate the contact either as a customer or a vendor with a straightforward checkbox option.

Filter records based on customers[Sales]

When users navigate to the Customers menu from Sales, they will exclusively view records filtered by the customers.

Filter records based on customers[Account]

When users navigate to the Customers menu from Accounts/Invoicing, they will exclusively view records filtered by the customers.

Filter records based on vendors[Account]

When users navigate to the Vendors menu from Accounts/Invoicing, they will exclusively view records filtered by the vendors.
